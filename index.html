Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of a professionally written resume

When you work with a resume writing service, you’re investing in a laser job search and a more productive and satisfying career. To have job search success, your resume has to appeal to both the resume, resume scanning software, and the hiring manager — otherwise you risk getting lost in the “resume black hole”, where your job application is dismissed without consideration because of your resume.

How does the resume-writing process work?

The resume writing process begins with you selecting a suitable order package according to your career goal, we then get to know you and learn about your history and career goals. Once we’ve learned more about you, we’ll match you with a certified writer knowledgeable about your industry. Your writer collaborates with you on a first draft. You’ll then have about a week to provide feedback to your writer, with an unlimited rounds of revisions. Overall, the resume writing process typically takes 3-5 working days turnaround.

What qualifications do Congentresume writers have?

We have a writer network that includes certified career coaches, former and current recruiters, and professionals with specialized experience in over 65 industries from finance to warehouse. This ensures we can pair each client with someone who is a strong match for their needs. Only 3% of candidates who apply to be a Congentresume writer are hired; Congentresume carefully recruits and vets our writers based upon the value they can offer to our clients..
